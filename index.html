Sabrina Carpenter

Sabrina Annlynn Carpenter is an American singer, songwriter, and actress who has captivated audiences since her early years. Known for her remarkable talent and versatility, she began her journey as a young Disney star and has since evolved into a successful pop artist. Her music resonates with millions, blending elements of pop, R&B, and folk.
